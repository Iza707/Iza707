ABOUT US

FLASH EXPRESS COMPANY BACKGROUND:

Founded by Miranda, Alejandro and Macalalad, students under BSIT from the Technological Institute of the Philippines, based in Quezon City, Manila, in 2023, Flash Express is an Asia-Region based delivery service dedicated to superb shipping services of goods from and to Asian countries. Our company offers affordable rates with quality services, diversity of Asia-based products, and 24/7 Customer Service.



OUR MISSION:

Our main objective is to seek ways to improve customer services, satisfaction, reduce transportation costs, and eliminate factors that cause inefficiency, and also Flash Express strives to improve logistics and transport operations.
